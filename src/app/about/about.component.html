Established in 1948, the International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience (IAESTE) is a worldwide organisation with over 80 member countries. It offers international traineeships for students studying degrees in science, engineering and related disciplines. IAESTE is the largest organisation of its kind in the world and aims to promote international understanding and goodwill amongst students, academic institutions, employers and the wider community
